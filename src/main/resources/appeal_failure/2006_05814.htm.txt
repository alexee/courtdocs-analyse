	The People of the State of New York, Respondent,
v
Gandhi Guzman, Appellant.


—[*1]Application by the appellant for a writ of error coram nobis to vacate, on the ground of ineffective assistance of appellate counsel, a decision and order of this Court dated December 27, 1999 (People v Guzman, 267 AD2d 471 [1999], cert denied 540 US 1119 [2004]), affirming a judgment of the Supreme Court, Queens County, rendered April 9, 1992.

Ordered that the application is denied.

The appellant has failed to establish that he was denied the effective assistance of appellate counsel (see Jones v Barnes, 463 US 745 [1983]; People v Stultz, 2 NY3d 277 [2004]). Prudenti, P.J., Florio, Schmidt and Krausman, JJ., concur.
